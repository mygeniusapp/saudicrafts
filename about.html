Our Story

SaudiCrafts is the Saudi online marketplace connecting local creators with customers who value original, handmade Saudi craftsmanship. Our mission is to simplify selling and buying unique products and preserve traditional Saudi arts in the digital market

Our Vision

To be the Number One destination in Saudi Arabia for authentic handmade and artistic products. We aim to empower every local artisan to thrive and spread the beauty of Saudi craftsmanship globally and locally

Our Mission

To build a safe, organized, and enjoyable shopping experience that highlights the quality of crafts. We commit to an easy browsing and purchasing process for customers, supported by professional display tools and digital training for artisans

Our Contribution

We support Saudi Vision 2030 by boosting the local economy and empowering small entrepreneurs. We strengthen cultural identity by preserving traditional Saudi arts and moving them into the sustainable digital world
